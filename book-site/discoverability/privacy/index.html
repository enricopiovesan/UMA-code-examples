Privacy Policy

This site collects minimal data. Here is what is collected, why, and how you can opt out.

What is collected

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to measure aggregate traffic: page views, session duration, and referral sources. No personally identifiable information is collected. IP addresses are anonymized. No advertising or retargeting data is used.

No accounts, logins, or forms exist on this site. No cookies beyond the Google Analytics measurement cookie are set.

Third-party services

Google Analytics provides aggregate usage metrics. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google Fonts provides typefaces loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log the request IP. See Google Fonts privacy FAQ.

Amazon: the "Pre-order on Amazon" links go to amazon.com. Amazon's own privacy policy applies when you visit their site.

Your rights

If you are in the EU or UK, you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, or delete data held about you. Because this site collects no personal data directly, there is nothing to access or delete beyond what Google Analytics retains. For Google's data deletion tools, visit Google Account: Data & Privacy.

Contact

Questions about this policy: reach Enrico on LinkedIn.
