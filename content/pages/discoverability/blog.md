  <section>
    <h2>What the writing covers</h2>
    <p>
      The articles follow the same progression as the book: starting with the problem (why distributed systems fragment
      as execution surfaces multiply), through the structural model (portable services, active descriptors, runtime
      governance), and into specific application areas (WebAssembly portability, AI-native runtime governance, MCP
      integration).
    </p>
    <p>
      Research papers that predated the book are also available: CSMA (June 2023), UMA (August 2024), and ECCA (August 2025).
      These document the model's development from initial composable service concept through the full UMA specification
      to execution context coherence for AI-assisted paths.
    </p>
